Who am I?

A drawn out answer to a simple question:

I am a 4th year student at Carleton University and I love tech and making things.

I started off making videos, and did that most of my childhood from ages 9-18. I was (am) a big time gamer on a lot of different consoles. So most of my videos centered around that.

Even though I spent a lot of time at home making videos about/in games; at school, any time an oppurtunity reared its head, I made sure to make a video for it. Essays, assignments, projects, you name it.

You'd think the class would matter right? Surely there is a limit? Wrong. I made videos for History, Law, French, Civics & Careers, English, and even Social Studies.

In highschool as a huge video nerd, I got really involved with the Communications Technolgies department. It was there where I made some of my best--and especially funniest--stuff. My friends and I made sketches, "short films", and offputting ads. I used a lot of different technolgies, and had a lot of fun.
